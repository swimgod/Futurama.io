About Us

Goal

We envision a future where we are able to reap the large benefits of autonomous driving (safety, energy efficiency, time, etc.), but we realize that many more people are needed to make this a reality. We hope that this blog serves as a critical building block for individuals of all levels (especially those new to the space) looking to learn the basics of autonomous driving.

Origins

We have been studying the autonomous driving space for about a year and a half now. During this time, we were challenged by a plethora of technical whitepapers and various converging technologies in play within an autonomous driving system that it took several months before we were able to get a basic understanding. In contrast to learning other topics, we noticed autonomous driving was much more difficult to learn because of the nascence, breadth and depth of the technology and there is a steep learning curve for anyone interested in learning the field. We want to bridge this gap for those eager to learn but are as overwhelmed as we were when we first started. Therefore, we have spent the last several months working on a guide covering fundamental topics in autonomous driving that we hope you enjoy!

We are open to any and all feedback you may have and will do our best to reply to any questions.
